ProEstimate AI turns a single job-site photo into a fully costed estimate, branded proposal, and billable invoice — in the time it used to take to open a spreadsheet.

Built for contractors, remodelers, handypeople, and trade professionals who want to win more jobs with less paperwork. Snap a photo of the space, let the AI render a photoreal before-and-after preview, and the app automatically suggests materials and labor priced for your quality tier.

WHAT YOU CAN DO
• Capture photos on device and see a photoreal AI remodel preview in seconds
• Choose from nine project categories: Kitchen, Bathroom, Flooring, Roofing, Painting, Siding, Room Remodel, Exterior, and Custom
• Auto-generated materials and labor line items with Standard, Premium, and Luxury quality tiers
• Structured estimate editor with grouped line items, per-item markup, taxation, discounts, and a DIY mode for owner-operated jobs
• Branded proposals with a shareable client-approval link
• Invoices created from approved estimates, with partial-payment tracking and overdue reminders
• English and Spanish throughout
